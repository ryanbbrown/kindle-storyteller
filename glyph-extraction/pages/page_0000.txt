The entire city would persist in here,
isolated and protected from whatever he
did in the cosmere. One perfect place of
peace and love.

His secret. His dangerous, shameful

secret. Because in the end, although no
one could ever know it, there were things
even Taravangian—in a moment of pain
and passion—had refused to sacrifice.

He embraced his grandchildren,
weeping, and the power simmered. Hating
Dalinar Kholin.

For having been right.
